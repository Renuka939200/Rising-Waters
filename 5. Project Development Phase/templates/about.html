🌊 About Rising Waters

Rising Waters is an AI-powered Flood Prediction and Early Warning System designed to predict potential flood events using Machine Learning techniques. The system analyses important meteorological parameters including annual rainfall, monsoon rainfall, river level, cloud coverage, humidity, and temperature to estimate flood risk. By providing early predictions, the application assists government agencies and disaster management teams in taking timely preventive actions, reducing risks, and improving emergency preparedness.

🎯 Project Objectives

  • Predict flood risk using Machine Learning algorithms.
  • Provide early warning for flood-prone regions.
  • Support disaster management authorities.
  • Improve emergency preparedness and planning.
  • Enable faster and data-driven decision making.
